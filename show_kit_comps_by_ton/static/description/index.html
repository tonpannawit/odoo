Display components in BOM on Website

Works on both Odoo Community & Enterprise

Products with BOM

Products with Bills of Materials(BOM) are either in Kit or Manufactured. Image below shows a product called "Table Kit" whose BOM is present.

Bills of Materials(BOM)

Once clicked, BOM displays a list of components in a kit or what components go into manufacturing. Website customers will be able to see these components listed on the website as well.

Website Display

A list of components along with individual product image and quantity per kit are displayed. This helps clear up any questions a user might have and may save you a phone call or an email asking a non-revenue generating question that can be automatically answered.

Transparency of information reduces resistance to purchase!

Upselling & Cross Selling Opportunities

If a product is published for sale on the website, its name in the component list will become a clickable link to that particular product's page enabling the user to conveniently add the product to cart.
